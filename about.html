About Me

Data Scientist passionate about building intelligent systems

Hello! I'm Satya Praneeth Mallipam

I am a Master's student in Data Science at Kent State University under the Computer Science Department, graduating in May 2026.

I specialize in Machine Learning, Data Engineering, Statistical Modeling, Data Pipelines, Big Data Analytics, and Visualization. My work focuses on transforming complex data into actionable insights and building intelligent systems that drive business value.

With strong technical experience in Python, R, SQL, JavaScript, React, D3.js, Tableau, and cloud technologies, I've developed a comprehensive skill set that bridges the gap between data science theory and practical implementation.

I have built several academic and industry-oriented projects with real-world datasets from sources including the U.S. Census, Bureau of Labor Statistics (BLS), Yelp, NOAA, and Kaggle. These projects demonstrate my ability to work with diverse data sources and extract meaningful patterns from complex datasets.

My interests include building data-driven products and intelligent systems that solve real business problems. I'm particularly passionate about:

  • Predictive modeling for business intelligence
  • Building scalable data pipelines and ETL systems
  • Creating interactive data visualizations
  • Developing machine learning solutions for real-world challenges
  • Statistical analysis and experimental design
